Why not allow people to post comments?
Spam, and because this is my own little part of the web. You can still comment in Twitter and through email. Better yet, I encourage you to start your own blog, link to my post, and comment away.

May I pay to write a guest post?
I used to say "sure, as long as I write that you that you paid for it". Funnily enough, unsolicited advertisers drop pretty fast after that.

Yeah, someone solving a problem is just the worst.
