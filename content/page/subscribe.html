Rubénerd is a blog and podcast. Choose your poison below.

Subscribe to the podcast

You can get the latest Rubénerd Show episodes delivered to your podcast client with the following feed:

http://rubenerd.com/show/feed/

You can also get through iTunes, which will set everything up for you. If you used iTunes prior to 2009, you may need to unsubscribe and re–add the show to fix a broken redirect.

Subscribe to the blog

Rubénerd also publishes a feed for posts. Like an email address, you can use it to receive updates in a blog reader. I recommend the relaunched online Digg Reader, or NetNewsWire on the Mac.

If you have a desktop blog reader, you should be able to click here:

feed://rubenerd.com/feed/

Or to add manually, copy and paste the following address:

http://rubenerd.com/feed/

If you see your online blog reader below, click to subscribe:

  • Bloglines
  • Feedbin
  • Feedly
  • My Yahoo!
  • Netvibes

You can also follow these Twitter accounts:

  • @Rubenerd_Blog for new posts
  • @Rubenerd for my personal ramblings and links

The following pages provide help with using RSS feeds:

  • Mozilla Thunderbird
  • Office Support (Outlook and Internet Explorer)

What happened to the newsletter?

Since 2009, they've ceased. They were being flagged for spam, and it was too much work getting them whitelisted on the likes of Gmail. If the demand is there in the future, I'll look into something like Mailchimp.
