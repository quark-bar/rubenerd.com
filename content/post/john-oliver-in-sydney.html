Yesterday evening, the Sydney Morning Herald ran this:

Desperate fans fork out for tickets

They could have only been talking about John Oliver’s Australia tour. How Clara and I kept refreshing that Ticketmaster page, in vain attempts to secure seats to see that star of The Daily Show and Last Week Tonight perform in Sydney. We were even putting thought into how we'd print some Jeff the Diseased Lung shirts to wear.

But, despite spending more time and effort into pre–ordering this experience than some throwaway electronic device (that people keep asking me whether I've got or not), the tickets was not to be.

The A-League grand final is being held at its smallest venue yet. Scalpers are cashing in.

Oh, that’s what the article was discussing? How about (between you and me), we pretend it was regarding Mr Oliver. It’ll make coping easier.
