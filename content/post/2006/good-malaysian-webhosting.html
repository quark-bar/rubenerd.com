I guess two questions are coming up:

Q: Why are you looking at webhosts when you have one already?
A: Servage's upload and FTP speeds are HORRIBLE… which for podcasting and blogging is impossible!

Q: Okay, so why a Malaysian webhost?
A: The rest of my nuclear family lives in Malaysia and if I was still in high school I would be there too. Plus South East Asians KNOW computers! Oh yeah, and the Ringgit exchange rate is VERY appealing!

So far Exabytes looks the best. 5GiB disk space, 50GiB of bandwidth… RM149 a year!
