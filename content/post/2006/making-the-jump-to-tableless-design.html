Really well-designed, easy to learn tutorial about how to create an entire site without tables. I like it because the step by step instructions and diagrams are presented like an online slideshow. Teaches you how to make navigation menus, columns, headers, footers etc. Worth checking out.

Making the Jump to Tableless Design by Andy Budd
