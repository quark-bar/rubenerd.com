So I'm sitting here watching television with my parents here in KL. We've got cable here which is refeshing after coping with public access television in Australia (okay, it's only Channel 10 that I have an issue with, but you get the idea).

One problem though, my mum just channel surfed and skipped what looked liked a fascinating documentary about how they built the Petronas Twin Towers here in KL, and a show about the composition of comets and how our solar system formed, to instead watching a show about sea horses.

I guess you can't win them all :(.
