The Rubénerd Show is a slice-of-life podcast by Ruben Schade. This excursion was recorded in Hornsby, in northern Sydney, Australia.

Topics include Hornsby, Singapore, New York City, the northern suburbs of Sydney, BBEdit, small business “startups”, commutes, Sydney Trains, Singapore MRT, scary parking, air suspension on Range Rovers, Australian Aboriginal names, Malaysia, Kuala Lumpur, New names, the VW Beetle, COEs, wanderlust, living out of boxes, The Overnightscape, the alleged Podcast Renaissance, discovering parks, and choosing alternate diverging time travel paths.
