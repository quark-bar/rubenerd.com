I use half a dozen languages for work and fun, but when I need to quickly get stuff done, Perl still does the job. I couldn't imagine my life without it.

#!/usr/bin/env perl -w
use strict;

&birthday("25th");

sub birthday {
    print("Happy $_[0] birthday!n");
}
