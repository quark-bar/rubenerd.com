--- title: "Find PostgreSQL data dir from PostgreSQL" date: "2014-10-28T00:38:49+00:00" source: "https://rubenerd.tumblr.com/post/101131104410" year: "2014" category: Museum tag: - from-the-rubenerd-annexe - sql ---

This post originally appeared on the Annexe.

Assuming you can access your database:

postgres=# SHOW data_directory

On Debian Wheezy with the official PostgreSQL repo, the default will show:

    data_directory
---
 /var/lib/postgresql/9.3/main
(1 row)

Too easy :).
