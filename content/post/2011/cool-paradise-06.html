In our continuing series of fascinating lyrical anecdotes derived from Ben Sidran’s 1990 album Cool Paradise, today Ben laments his experience with banks and other such financial institutions in the tune Language of The Blues:

You can almost hear them say…
"Ask… and we shall refuse"…
In the language of the blues

At least, that’s what I think he’s talking about. Makes sense.
