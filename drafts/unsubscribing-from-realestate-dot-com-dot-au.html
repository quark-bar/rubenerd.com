--- title: "Unsubscribing from Realestate.com.au" date: "2015-09-21T14:41:00+10:00" ---

The ease (or lack thereof) of unsubscribing to newsletters is a recurring theme here. It’s such fertile ground.

Today's message comes from RealEstate.com.au:

You have been unsubscribed from receiving product announcement emails.

We may still send you other marketing communications from time to time.

Yikes. Unsubscribe means unsubscribe, not “mostly” unsubscribe. Fortunately, they had a “manage your email preferences link”, which took me to a login page, then an Email preferences screen with the follwing links.

Newsletters
Welcome & Member Emails
Property journey tips and guides
Market Announcements
Realestate.com.au announcements
Partner Offers
Saves Search Alerts

There was no select all. Eight clicks, including the two from before, to unsubscribe.

Unsubscribe link? Action Score
Yes Unsubscribes A+
Yes Asks for confirmation C
Yes Requires login, then unsubscribes D
Yes Requires login, then find unsubscribe page E
No n/a F
