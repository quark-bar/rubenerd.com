If you peruse the Xfce site, you'll see the screenshots include a squarish icon theme. It's not the Mouse or Tango icons that package managers and distros generally include by default with their Xfce desktops.

Turns out, it's Faenza. From Wikipedia:

Faenza (pronounced [faˈɛntsa]) is an Italian city and comune, in the province of Ravenna, Emilia-Romagna, situated 50 kilometres (31 miles) southeast of Bologna.

Faenza is noted for its manufacture of majolica ware glazed earthenware pottery, known from the name of the town as "faience".

Well, that's clearly the wrong one. Still, I learned something today.
